About GreenEO

GreenEO empowers decision-makers with advanced satellite-based tools to manage land use, monitor pollution, and protect biodiversity.By integrating next-gen Earth observation (EO) data with climate models, digital infrastructure, and machine learning, the project supports transformative governance aligned with the European Green Deal.
